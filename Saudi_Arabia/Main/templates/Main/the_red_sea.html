The Red Sea Project

A luxury regenerative tourism destination along Saudi Arabia's west coast — where pristine nature meets sustainable innovation.

Where Nature Inspires the Future

The Red Sea Project is one of the world's most ambitious regenerative tourism developments. Built across over 28,000 square kilometers of untouched islands, deserts, and mountains, it will set new standards for sustainable luxury travel — protecting the environment while offering unforgettable experiences.

Key Highlights

90+ Islands

Carefully preserved natural habitats and coral reefs.

100% Renewable

Powered entirely by clean solar and wind energy.

1 Million Visitors

Expected annually, with strict sustainability limits.

50+ Resorts

Ultra-luxury hospitality blending modern comfort with nature.

Shaping a Global Tourism Landmark

The Red Sea Project will redefine how destinations coexist with nature. With world-class architecture, crystal-clear waters, and a commitment to environmental balance, it stands as a cornerstone of Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 — a model of harmony between progress and preservation.
