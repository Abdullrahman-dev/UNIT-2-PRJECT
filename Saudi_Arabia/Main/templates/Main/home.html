Vision 2030: Shaping the Future of Saudi Arabia

Saudi Vision 2030 is a national transformation plan that aims to diversify the Kingdom's economy, promote innovation, and enhance the quality of life. Through ambitious projects like NEOM, Qiddiya, and The Red Sea, Saudi Arabia is building a future driven by opportunity and creativity.

About Vision 2030

Sustainability

Saudi Vision 2030 promotes a greener future by investing in renewable energy, protecting natural resources, and supporting eco-friendly development across the Kingdom.

Economy Growth

The Vision aims to create new job opportunities, attract global investments, and empower local industries to build a thriving and diversified economy.

Innovation & Technology

Saudi Arabia is investing in research, smart cities, and cutting-edge technologies to drive digital transformation and inspire global innovation.

Featured Projects

NEOM

A futuristic city built on innovation, sustainability, and technology — redefining urban living for the next generation.

The Line

A 170 km-long linear city with zero cars, zero streets, and zero carbon emissions — designed for a sustainable future.

The Red Sea

A world-class luxury destination combining nature, adventure, and sustainability — redefining global tourism in Saudi Arabia.
