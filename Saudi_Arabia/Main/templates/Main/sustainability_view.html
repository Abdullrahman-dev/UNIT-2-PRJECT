Sustainability In Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is leading a sustainable transformation under Vision 2030 — focusing on clean energy, environmental protection, and preserving nature for future generations.

Renewable Energy

Saudi Arabia is one of the world's fastest-growing renewable energy leaders. Through solar, wind, and hydrogen projects — such as NEOM's clean energy initiatives — the Kingdom is reducing carbon emissions and diversifying its energy sources.

Environmental Protection

Vision 2030 promotes preserving Saudi Arabia's natural landscapes — from deserts to coastlines — through reforestation, waste management, and biodiversity programs.
