About Vision 2030

Transforming ambition into action for a prosperous and sustainable future.

The Vision

Saudi Vision 2030 is a bold blueprint for the Kingdom's future, launched to diversify the economy, enhance quality of life, and create opportunities for citizens and residents alike. The Vision focuses on innovation, sustainability, and global leadership.

Our Goals

  • Build a thriving economy driven by innovation and technology.
  • Empower Saudi youth to lead globally in science, culture, and entrepreneurship.
  • Preserve the environment through sustainable projects and renewable energy.
  • Enhance quality of life for all residents and visitors.

Core Values

Innovation

Driving progress through creativity and cutting-edge technology.

Sustainability

Building an environmentally responsible and balanced future.

Excellence

Achieving world-class standards in all sectors.
