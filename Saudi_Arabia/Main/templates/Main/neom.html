NEOM

Welcome to NEOM

A bold vision for the future — NEOM represents Saudi Arabia's ambition to redefine sustainable living, technology, and innovation on a global scale. Designed to be a beacon of progress, NEOM is more than just a city — it's a blueprint for tomorrow.

OXAGON

OXAGON is the world's largest floating industrial city and the industrial heart of NEOM. It's designed to be a global hub for advanced manufacturing, clean energy, logistics, and technology — all powered by renewable energy.

TROJENA

TROJENA is NEOM's futuristic mountain resort — where nature, adventure, and luxury meet. It's the first outdoor ski destination in the Arabian Peninsula, blending modern architecture with breathtaking mountain landscapes.

NEOM is built around human well-being — walkable communities, green spaces, and AI-enhanced services for a better quality of life.
